Cincinnati 311: Focused Service Requests

Interactive map and charts for six 311 categories (potholes, plumbing, metal furniture, sewage, graffiti, recycling). Live data covers about 2004-2026; 2026 is partial (through March 21). Earliest years have very few records.

    Data scope

    Requests over time

    •

    All years: monthly counts. Choosing a year switches to weekly counts and filters the map and charts to that year. Drag on the chart to narrow the date range further. Compare full calendar years only.

    Select on the map

    Draw region then drag on the map to define a filter. Use Move region to drag the box without panning the map. Press Esc to cancel drawing or move mode.
